Features

Everything you need for distributed training

No cloud required. No complex setup. Just MacBooks and WiFi.

Zero-Config Discovery
Devices find each other automatically via mDNS — the same protocol behind AirDrop and AirPlay.
500x Less Network Traffic
DiLoCo syncs once every 500 steps instead of every step. WiFi is more than fast enough.
Fault Tolerant
Nodes can join and leave mid-training without killing the run. The swarm adapts automatically.
Training Relay
Pause training, export a checkpoint, hand it off to someone else. Like a relay race for AI.
Apple Silicon Native
Built on MLX — Apple's ML framework. Unified memory means no host-to-device copy overhead.
Community Platform
Find training partners, share checkpoints, and track contributions on a global leaderboard.

The Algorithm

DiLoCo: Distributed Low-Communication Learning

Each Mac trains independently, then syncs only the difference. The math is elegant.

Inner Loop (local training)
/* Each worker independently */
θ_local = θ_global
for step in range(500):
    loss = model(batch, θ_local)
    θ_local -= α · AdamW(∇loss)  // α = 3e-4
Outer Loop (synchronization)
/* Coordinator averages pseudo-gradients */
Δθ = θ_global - θ_local       // pseudo-gradient
Δθ_avg = mean(Δθ_1 ... Δθ_n)

/* Outer SGD + Nesterov momentum */
v = 0.9 · v + Δθ_avg
θ_global -= 0.7 · (Δθ_avg + 0.9 · v)
500x
Less communication than DDP. Traditional distributed training syncs ~500MB every step. AirTrain syncs once every 500 steps — making WiFi viable.

Sleep Swarms

Your Mac trains while you sleep. Set a window, and AirTrain auto-joins sessions overnight — then hands off to someone in another timezone when you wake up. The model trains 24/7 by chasing nighttime around the globe.

Set Your Window

Tell AirTrain when your Mac is free. It handles the rest — finding sessions, downloading checkpoints, and training automatically.

airtrain sleep --window "23:00-07:00"

Chase the Night

New York sleeps, Tokyo wakes. Your checkpoint passes across time zones so the model never stops training. 24/7 compute from idle MacBooks worldwide.

Smart Matching

Filter by model preference with --prefer "gpt2*". AirTrain matches you to sessions that need your timezone's coverage the most.

Safe by Default

Auto-stops if battery drops below 20%, lid is closed, or your window ends. Checkpoints are saved before every disconnect. Zero data loss.

Dream Training

Your Mac generates synthetic training data during idle time — running inference on the current model, scoring outputs for quality, and caching the best samples. When training resumes, dream data is mixed into real batches to accelerate convergence.
